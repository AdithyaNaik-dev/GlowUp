Privacy Policy for GlowUp

Effective Date: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GlowUp ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of information necessary to provide a great experience:

  • Account Information: When you sign in via Google Sign-In or Email, we securely collect your email address, display name, and profile photo URL.
  • Camera & Facial Data: We use your device camera locally for AI workout tracking. Your facial/body landmarks are processed entirely on-device in real-time. We DO NOT record, store, or transmit your video feed or facial data to our servers or any third parties.
  • Usage & Progress Data: We collect your workout progress, current streak, points earned, and daily activity logs to power the Gamification and Leaderboard systems via Firebase Firestore.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to operate the app:

  • To manage your account via Firebase Authentication.
  • To display your rank, name, and score on the public leaderboard.
  • To securely synchronize your progress across multiple devices.

3. Third-Party Services

We use industry-standard third-party services that may collect data as governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In: To authenticate you securely without us ever seeing your password.
  • Firebase (Auth & Firestore): To host our backend database and authentication securely.
  • AdMob: To serve advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers to personalize ads.

4. Data Selling

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for their direct marketing purposes. Your data remains yours and is only used to provide the GlowUp experience.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@glowupapp.com (or your actual contact email).
